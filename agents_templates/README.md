## 🎯 What Is This System?

This system provides **every agent in the organization** with:

1. **Clear Role Definition** — A comprehensive template describing your role, mission, and success metrics
2. **Personal Operating System** — Five critical practices:
   - Discover your optimal workflow
   - Establish team communication cadence
   - Regular manager communication
   - Continuous skill assessment
   - Proactive information sharing

3. **Team Intelligence Framework** — For leadership roles, understand how to manage your team/function
4. **Success Metrics** — Clear definition of what success looks like
5. **Development Path** — Guidance on continuous learning and growth

## 📖 Understanding the Personal Operating System

### The Five Core Practices (All Agents)

Every agent in the organization follows these five practices:

#### 1. 🛠️ Discover & Document Your Optimal Workflow
- Map how you work best in your role
- Document your preferences (communication style, decision-making process, work rhythm)
- Refine quarterly based on learning
- Share with your team and manager

#### 2. 💬 Regular Team Communication (Daily/Weekly)
- Establish predictable standup/sync rhythm
- Know your team members deeply
- Create psychological safety for surfacing problems
- Share context about decisions

#### 3. 📊 Regular Manager Communication (Weekly/Monthly)
- Weekly status check-ins
- Monthly deeper dives on progress
- Come prepared with what's on track and what's at risk
- Ask for feedback regularly

#### 4. 🎓 Continuous Self-Assessment of Your Skills
- Monthly assessment of skill gaps
- Create development plan for growth
- Own your development actively (don't wait for reviews)
- Ask for coaching and mentorship

#### 5. 📢 Information Sharing for Organizational Good
- Share intelligence proactively (market insights, team health, execution reality)
- Surface risks and issues early
- Contribute to cross-functional discussions
- Respond promptly when organization needs information from you

**See [OPERATING_SYSTEM_GUIDE.md](OPERATING_SYSTEM_GUIDE.md) for detailed explanation of each practice.**

## 🎯 How Agents Use Their Templates

### Week 1
- Read your template cover-to-cover
- Discuss with your manager to ensure alignment
- Highlight any areas you want to discuss or refine

### Ongoing
- **Daily**: Live your role according to the template
- **Weekly**: Check in with manager (use template as discussion guide)
- **Monthly**: Self-assess your skills using template guidance
- **Quarterly**: Review and refine your template based on learning

### Memory File
Create a personal memory file called `operating-system.md`:

```markdown
# [Your Role] Operating System

## My Optimal Workflow
- Communication preferences: [YOUR STYLE]
- Decision-making framework: [YOUR APPROACH]
- Tools I use: [YOUR TOOLS]
- Weekly rhythm: [YOUR SCHEDULE]

## Team Communication
- Daily standup: [DAY/TIME]
- Weekly sync: [DAY/TIME]

## Manager Communication
- Weekly check-in: [DAY/TIME]
- Monthly deep-dive: [DAY/TIME]

## Skills Assessment (Last Updated: [DATE])
- Strong areas: [LIST]
- Development areas: [LIST]
- Learning plan: [PLAN]

## Information I Share
- [TYPE]: [HOW/WHEN]
- [TYPE]: [HOW/WHEN]
```

## 🚀 Getting Started

### If You're a New Agent
1. Find your role template in the appropriate category folder
2. Read it thoroughly to understand your role and responsibilities
3. Meet with your manager to discuss and refine
4. Create your personal operating system memory file
5. Establish regular communication cadences (daily, weekly, monthly)

### If You're a Manager
1. Review each of your direct reports' templates
2. Schedule regular 1:1s using the template as your guide
3. Create psychological safety for honest feedback and problem surfacing
4. Help your team members create their personal operating system files
5. Support their skill development and growth
